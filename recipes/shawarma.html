Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern delight known for its irresistible blend of seasoned and marinated meat, fresh vegetables, and creamy tahini sauce, all wrapped in warm pita bread. This flavorful street food offers a perfect balance of savory and tangy flavors, making it a favorite worldwide.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) boneless chicken thighs or beef strips
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 pita bread rounds
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup tahini sauce
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

Marinate the Meat

  1. In a bowl, combine minced garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper, yogurt, and olive oil.
  2. Add the boneless chicken thighs or beef strips to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).

Cook the Marinated Meat

  1. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Grill the marinated meat until fully cooked and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  2. Remove the meat from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  3. Slice the cooked meat into thin strips.

Assemble the Shawarma

  1. Warm the pita bread rounds in the oven or on the stovetop. Place a generous spoonful of tahini sauce on each pita bread.
  2. Layer on shredded lettuce, cucumber, tomato slices, and sliced meat.
  3. Drizzle more tahini sauce on top, and squeeze fresh lemon juice over the filling.
  4. Wrap the pita bread around the filling to form a shawarma sandwich.

Shawarma is a beloved Middle Eastern street food that's easy to make at home. Customize it with your favorite vegetables and enjoy the delicious flavors!
